I. Platform Background & Mission

Project Overview

Truoux is a next-generation digital asset trading platform designed for global users, committed to lowering the barriers to market participation and reshaping the user experience. The platform's core pillars are strategy tools, modular architecture, and a progressive growth guidance system — designed to help users advance from entry-level to advanced trading in the crypto asset space.

The design philosophy emphasizes structural simplicity, clear user pathways, and friendly interactions. Combining visual guidance with behavioral assistance mechanisms, Truoux helps users quickly master trading logic and key competencies. The existing ecosystem covers core segments: spot trading, derivatives, asset management, and educational support — building an inclusive and high-growth digital asset participation system oriented toward emerging markets.

Market Background

As the digital asset market's user base continues to expand and diversify — particularly in emerging market regions — trading platforms face increasingly demanding requirements for adaptability and product quality. Current mainstream platforms commonly exhibit pain points: high barriers to entry, complex processes, weak language localization, and missing educational support. This leaves a large number of users unable to complete smooth trades on their first attempt.

Truoux was founded in April 2019 in the United States against this backdrop, with the mission to build a digital asset trading platform that meets the needs of the new generation of users.

🏛️
Platform Mission: Truoux's mission is to build a genuinely accessible digital asset trading platform for everyday users — one that lowers barriers and encourages growth. Through optimized registration, multi-language support, and simplified operations, users can more easily understand trading mechanics and progressively build asset management capabilities.

III. Platform Positioning & Characteristics

Inclusive Trading Platform

Truoux positions itself as an inclusive digital asset platform, extending the concept of accessibility from merely "low barrier to entry" to a full-cycle, full-scenario system. This spans trading, strategy, education, and value circulation across the entire ecosystem.

  • Low-Barrier Access — One-click registration, flexible login, and multi-language environment to shorten first-time onboarding time.
  • Cognitive Guidance — Graphical operation pathways combined with distributed guidance to reduce understanding and execution costs.
  • Intelligent Protection — AI behavioral recognition system monitors anomalies in real time and automatically triggers security responses.
  • Progressive Growth — Matching trading trajectories to growth curves, building a one-stop professional advancement pathway.

Core Platform Capabilities

Wealth Inclusion Engine: Aggregates mainstream and emerging digital assets, connecting multiple trade types and cross-chain products to eliminate asset and liquidity barriers. Intelligent allocation and dynamic rebalancing optimize structure, while a cognition-driven model shapes a universal wealth circulation network.

Cross-Domain Collaborative Ecosystem: Integrates internal trading depth with external protocol resources to provide low-cost, high-efficiency matching for all asset types. An invisible swap mechanism enables low-latency conversion across asset categories, with acceleration channels for quality emerging projects.

Intelligent Trading Hub: Scene-adaptive at its core — the trading interface flexibly switches between use cases and strategy requirements. It optimizes order execution paths and liquidity matching for efficient, scalable trading.

Self-Evolving Security Domain: An iterative security system that captures and maps potential threats in real time, with millisecond response to emergent risks. By recording and analyzing historical attack patterns, it automatically reinforces defenses during high-volatility and extreme events.

User Empowerment Growth: Multi-tier navigation converts learning, trading, and other behaviors into usable rights, forming a positive growth loop. Immersive governance mechanisms let participants gain quantifiable outcomes from the learning process.

IV. Technology Architecture & Security

Architecture Overview

Truoux's underlying technology architecture is built on principles of high availability, scalability, and modular deployment. The platform core consists of a matching engine, account system, risk control module, data service layer, and API gateway — supported by real-time monitoring and disaster recovery mechanisms to ensure trading continuity and platform stability under high-concurrency scenarios.

The system adopts a microservices architecture with all modules communicating asynchronously via message queues, maintaining optimal balance between performance and security. The platform supports multi-node deployment with automatic failover for 7×24 high-availability operation.

V. Compliance & Regulatory Qualifications

Truoux treats compliance as a core pillar of platform strategic development, proactively aligning with mainstream international regulatory frameworks to ensure all business activities operate within legal boundaries.

Truoux's compliance commitment encompasses transparent, traceable operations under regulatory oversight — demonstrating a 'safe, compliant, and sustainable' core promise to global users, ensuring every transaction operates within a trusted institutional framework.

US MSB License (FinCEN)

Truoux holds a Money Services Business (MSB) registration issued by the US Treasury's Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN). This license provides the compliance basis for virtual currency exchange and transfer services in the United States and other jurisdictions, ensuring user asset transactions are protected under anti-money laundering regulations. MSB registration information is publicly accessible, covering customer identity recognition, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting, and fund flow records.

SEC Regulation D Qualification

Truoux has obtained a Regulation D exemption qualification from the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) — a milestone achieved in September 2025. This compliance credential enables the platform to safely and legally provide digital asset products and services within a strict legal and regulatory framework, providing global users with a more secure and trustworthy environment for digital asset market participation.

AML & CTF Compliance Framework

  • KYC (Know Your Customer) real-name authentication and address verification processes
  • Transaction behavior monitoring and high-risk address identification mechanisms
  • Suspicious Transaction Reports (STRs) filing system
  • Third-party compliance vendor collaboration including on-chain behavior analysis and sanction list synchronization
  • AI anomaly recognition system integrated across all compliance modules
  • GDPR and CCPA data privacy compliance with end-to-end encrypted transmission
