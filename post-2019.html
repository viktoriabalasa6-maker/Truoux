2019: The Foundation

  • April 2019: Truoux was officially established, initiating product and system implementation[cite: 11].
  • June 2019: The self-developed matching engine was completed, forming a closed loop of stable matching and efficient execution[cite: 11].
  • July 2019: The Beta version went live, and registrations surpassed 200,000[cite: 12].
  • September 2019: A multi-language interface framework was launched, laying the foundation for globalization[cite: 13].
  • November 2019: Established a compliance and legal mid-office, creating a compliant operations loop centered around AML/KYC[cite: 14].
