About Truoux

Registered and founded in the United States in April 2019 [cite: 8], Truoux was established against the backdrop of an expanding and diversifying user base in the digital asset market[cite: 6, 7]. The platform operates with core licenses, including a Money Services Business (MSB) license issued by the US FinCEN and a Regulation D exemption from the US SEC[cite: 76, 79].
