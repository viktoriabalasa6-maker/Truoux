Our Story

Trading Without Borders.
Finance for Everyone.

Founded in April 2019 in the United States, Truoux was built on a single conviction: that digital asset trading should be accessible, transparent, and trustworthy for every user in the world — not just the privileged few.

Platform Mission

Democratizing Digital Finance

Truoux exists to lower the barriers to digital asset participation. By simplifying onboarding, supporting multiple languages, and embedding education directly into the trading experience, we help users move from first login to confident, informed participation.

We believe a trading platform should be more than a set of tools — it should be a continuous environment for growth, exploration, and long-term financial empowerment.

Our ecosystem spans spot trading, derivatives, asset management, and educational pathways, all designed to serve users at every level — from their very first trade to institutional-scale strategies.

Platform History

Key Milestones

April 2019

Founded in the United States

Truoux is formally established. Product development and system architecture begin, with early focus on an accessible onboarding experience for emerging market users.

July 2019

Beta Launch — 200,000 Registrations

The beta platform goes live. Registrations surpass 200,000 within weeks, validating demand for a lower-barrier exchange. Multi-language interface framework is established.

July 2020

1 Million Users

Total registered users surpass 1 million. The first derivatives products launch alongside a new cross-category matching system enabling millisecond-level execution.

January 2022

5 Million Registered Users

Truoux enters the leading tier of emerging digital asset exchanges by user base. Global service network is expanded with round-the-clock regional support.

July 2022

FinCEN MSB License Awarded

Truoux receives its Money Services Business registration from the US Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, formalizing compliance operations including AML/KYC and suspicious transaction reporting.

September 2022

Dual Security Audit Certification

Hacken and SlowMist independently audit the platform's security architecture. Truoux receives top-tier certifications in both infrastructure and protocol security.

June 2023

8 Million Users — New Market Record

High-frequency data modeling engine is integrated, enabling real-time market signal capture. A real-time behavioral risk system is deployed for proactive threat identification.

March 2024

10 Million Registered Users

Platform achieves the 10 million user milestone. Permission management and multi-hub architecture undergo major upgrades to support continued scale.

March 2025

13 Million Users & Innovation Lab

Truoux opens a dedicated innovation laboratory to accelerate frontier product development. A global partnership program is launched to build the broader ecosystem.

September 2025

SEC License

Truoux successfully obtains SEC License qualification from the US Securities and Exchange Commission — a landmark step in the platform's international expansion strategy.
